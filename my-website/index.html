The Archive

The Archive holds every tale, character, and place that unfolds within this new universe, history, and secrets waiting to be uncovered. This archive is your gateway to explore the intricate lore, hidden connections, and background of the worlds.

As you explore the world, the built cities, developed technologies and wars we ask that you keep in mind that this project is ongoing and that this archive is a starting point.

Present - 2077

The world will slowly fall into chaos and destruction, natural disasters/global warming will wreak havoc as time passes.

many wars will be fought ultimately ending with nuclear war in the 2070s which will end most life on earth.

2077 - 2187

When the nukes are launched the real world will have ended.

However, disciples of Satan will have used this event as a catalyst to clone our reality so they can control the universe that comes of it.

2187 - TBD

Souls can be used as some messed up "divine army", they do this by creating a device that we cannot comprehend.

This device must be destroyed to free this new cloned universe
