The Archive

The Archive of Polytopia is an award winning turn based strategy game about building a civilization and going into battle. With millions of players, it has quickly become one of the most popular mobile strategy games.

You play as one of many tribes, explore the world, build cities, develop technologies and wage war against other tribes in order to take control over the mythical land of Polytopia.
